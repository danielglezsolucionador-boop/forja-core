# 2.7 SHARED GOVERNANCE MODULES

## PURPOSE
Define reusable governance modules for controlled execution and approval flows.

## OBJECTIVES
- Prevent unsafe automation from bypassing human control.
- Establish approval gates for high-risk operations.
- Make execution decisions auditable.

## REQUIRED MODULES
- Scope declaration boundary.
- Approval request contract.
- Risk classification concept.
- Execution blocking signal.
- Audit trail metadata.

## RULES
- Do not allow generated apps to perform destructive actions without governance.
- Do not hide blocked states.
- Do not auto-approve sensitive operations.

## ECOSYSTEM COMPATIBILITY
- Governance modules must support FORJA automation, Hermes operations, and future app factory workflows.
- Approval contracts must remain human-readable.

## SUCCESS CONDITIONS
- Governance is reusable and explicit.
- Blocked operations are explainable.
- Audit trails can reconstruct decisions.
