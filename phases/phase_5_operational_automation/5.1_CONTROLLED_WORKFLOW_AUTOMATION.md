# 5.1 CONTROLLED WORKFLOW AUTOMATION

## PURPOSE
Define controlled workflow automation for FORJA without implementing automation yet.

## OBJECTIVES
- Establish how future workflows start, pause, resume, and stop.
- Keep automation behind explicit scope and approval.
- Avoid uncontrolled generation loops.

## REQUIRED COMPONENTS
- Workflow definition concept.
- Step state model.
- Human checkpoint boundary.
- Failure handling rule.
- Audit record concept.

## RULES
- Do not automate destructive actions without approval.
- Do not continue after critical errors.
- Do not hide workflow state from operators.

## ECOSYSTEM COMPATIBILITY
- Workflow automation must support FORJA-generated apps and ecosystem handoffs.
- Automation must remain compatible with governance and audit layers.

## SUCCESS CONDITIONS
- Workflow control is documented.
- Human checkpoints are explicit.
- Future automation can be tested incrementally.
