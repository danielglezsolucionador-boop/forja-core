# 5.5 OPERATIONAL RECOVERY SYSTEM

## PURPOSE
Define recovery expectations for FORJA workflows and generated outputs.

## OBJECTIVES
- Ensure failed workflows can stop safely.
- Preserve enough state for diagnosis.
- Support rollback and resume when authorized.

## REQUIRED COMPONENTS
- Failure classification.
- Checkpoint concept.
- Rollback metadata.
- Resume eligibility rule.
- Incident note concept.

## RULES
- Do not auto-resume after critical failures.
- Do not overwrite recovery evidence.
- Do not hide partial output state.

## ECOSYSTEM COMPATIBILITY
- Recovery metadata must support ecosystem audit and handoff processes.
- Generated apps should include recovery notes when delivered.

## SUCCESS CONDITIONS
- Recovery behavior is defined.
- Critical failures block unsafe continuation.
- Operators can understand what happened and what is recoverable.
