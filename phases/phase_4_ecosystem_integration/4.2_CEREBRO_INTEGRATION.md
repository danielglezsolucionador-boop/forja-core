# 4.2 CEREBRO INTEGRATION

## PURPOSE
Define FORJA compatibility with Cerebro as an ecosystem coordination layer.

## OBJECTIVES
- Support future coordination signals without coupling FORJA to Cerebro internals.
- Keep integration contract-based.
- Preserve FORJA as a factory, not the coordinator.

## REQUIRED COMPONENTS
- Coordination event concept.
- App metadata contract.
- Generated output handoff concept.
- Read-only status compatibility.
- Governance escalation boundary.

## RULES
- Do not make Cerebro mandatory for local FORJA documentation.
- Do not invent Cerebro APIs.
- Do not let coordination bypass FORJA validation.

## ECOSYSTEM COMPATIBILITY
- FORJA outputs should be describable to Cerebro later.
- Integration must stay modular and reversible.

## SUCCESS CONDITIONS
- Cerebro role is respected.
- Coordination boundaries are clear.
- No fake ecosystem dependency is created.
