# 4.6 ECOSYSTEM OUTPUT DISTRIBUTION

## PURPOSE
Define how FORJA outputs should be distributed across the ecosystem when authorized.

## OBJECTIVES
- Separate local creation from distribution.
- Define handoff expectations for generated apps, APIs, templates, and modules.
- Preserve rollback and traceability.

## REQUIRED COMPONENTS
- Output package metadata.
- Destination declaration.
- Validation evidence bundle.
- Rollback note.
- Owner handoff record.

## RULES
- Do not push, deploy, or publish without explicit authorization.
- Do not distribute incomplete outputs.
- Do not overwrite ecosystem assets without review.

## ECOSYSTEM COMPATIBILITY
- Distribution must support internal apps and client deliverables.
- Handoffs must be readable by operations, engineering, and governance.

## SUCCESS CONDITIONS
- Distribution is controlled.
- Outputs carry validation evidence.
- Rollback and ownership are clear.
